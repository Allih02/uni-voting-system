Need Help?

How do I log in to the voting system?
Use your university student ID and password to log in through the Student Login portal. If you're an administrator, use your admin credentials through the Administrator Login.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive password reset instructions on your university email address.
How do I cast my vote?
After logging in, you'll see available elections. Click on an election, select your candidate, and confirm your vote. You'll receive a confirmation message once your vote is recorded.
Is my vote confidential?
Yes, all votes are encrypted and anonymous. While the system verifies your eligibility to vote, it does not store any connection between your identity and your voting choice.
