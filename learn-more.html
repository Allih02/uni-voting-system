Everything You Need to Know About Our Voting System

A comprehensive guide to understanding how our secure and transparent election platform works

Registration

The first step is to create your account using your university email address. Our system verifies that you are an eligible voter by confirming your email domain matches the university's official domain.

During registration, you'll need to provide:

  • Your full name (as it appears in university records)
  • Student ID number
  • University email address
  • Department or faculty
  • Year of study

This information is encrypted and stored securely, and is only used to verify your eligibility to vote.

Verification

After registration, we implement a two-step verification process to ensure your identity:

  1. Email Verification: A confirmation link is sent to your university email. Clicking this link confirms you have access to the email account.
  2. Identity Verification: You'll need to provide a unique token that is either:
    • Sent directly to your student portal
    • Provided by your department administrator
    • Generated based on your student ID and a university-specific algorithm

This multi-factor authentication ensures that only eligible students can participate in the election, preventing impersonation and fraud.

Candidate Review

Once verified, you gain access to the candidate profiles section. Here, you can:

  • View detailed profiles of all candidates running for various positions
  • Read candidate manifestos and policy positions
  • Watch video presentations from candidates (where available)
  • Review candidates' past experience and qualifications
  • Compare candidates side-by-side on key issues

The platform presents information in a neutral, unbiased manner, giving equal prominence to all candidates. This helps you make an informed decision based on candidates' merits and platforms.

Voting

The voting interface is designed to be intuitive and accessible:

  1. Ballot Access: During the active election period, you'll see a prominent "Vote Now" button on your dashboard.
  2. Position Selection: Elections are organized by position. Select a position to see all candidates running for that role.
  3. Candidate Selection: Choose your preferred candidate by selecting the radio button next to their name. Some elections may use ranked-choice voting, allowing you to rank candidates in order of preference.
  4. Review Choices: Before final submission, you'll see a summary of all your selections for review.

The system is designed to prevent common voting errors, such as accidentally skipping positions or submitting incomplete ballots. You'll receive clear warnings if any part of your ballot is incomplete.

Confirmation

After submitting your vote:

  • You'll receive an immediate on-screen confirmation that your vote was recorded
  • A unique vote receipt code will be generated (keep this for verification purposes)
  • A confirmation email will be sent to your university email address
  • Your vote is encrypted and anonymized before being added to the blockchain

The confirmation process ensures that:

  • You have proof that you voted
  • Your vote was properly recorded
  • Your vote remains anonymous
  • Your vote cannot be altered or deleted

Results

Once the election period closes:

  • Results are tallied automatically by the system
  • Verification checks are performed to ensure accuracy
  • Results are published on the platform, including:
    • Total votes cast
    • Votes per candidate
    • Percentage of eligible voters who participated
    • Breakdown by department/faculty (where applicable)
  • Interactive visualizations show voting patterns and trends
  • Historical comparisons with previous elections

All results are verifiable against the blockchain record, ensuring complete transparency. External auditors can also verify the integrity of the election results using our public verification tool.

Our Technology

Security Architecture

Our system employs multiple layers of security to protect the integrity of the voting process:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted from the moment it leaves your browser until it's stored in our secure databases
  • Blockchain Verification: Each vote is recorded on a private blockchain, creating an immutable record that cannot be altered
  • Anonymization Layer: Your identity is separated from your vote using zero-knowledge proofs, ensuring complete anonymity
  • DDoS Protection: Advanced traffic filtering prevents denial-of-service attacks during election periods
  • Regular Security Audits: Independent cybersecurity firms audit our system before each election cycle

Verification System

Our verification system ensures election integrity while maintaining voter privacy:

  • Vote Receipts: Each voter receives a unique, anonymous receipt code
  • Public Bulletin Board: All encrypted votes are published on a public bulletin board
  • Cryptographic Proofs: Mathematical proofs verify votes without revealing voter identities
  • Open Source Components: Critical security components are open source for public review
  • Third-Party Validators: Independent election observers can verify results without compromising privacy

Accessibility Features

We've designed our system to be accessible to all eligible voters:

  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Full support for JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver
  • Keyboard Navigation: Complete functionality without requiring mouse input
  • High Contrast Mode: Enhanced visibility option for visually impaired users
  • Text-to-Speech: Built-in option to have candidate information read aloud
  • Responsive Design: Works on all devices, from desktops to mobile phones
  • Low Bandwidth Mode: Operates efficiently on slow internet connections

Analytics Dashboard

After the election, our analytics dashboard provides insights without compromising voter privacy:

  • Participation Metrics: Track voter turnout across departments and years
  • Trend Analysis: Compare current election results with historical data
  • Demographic Insights: Analyze voting patterns by academic year and faculty (where permitted)
  • Time-Based Analysis: See voting activity throughout the election period
  • Interactive Visualizations: Explore results through intuitive charts and graphs
