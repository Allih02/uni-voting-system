1. Introduction

1.1 About the University Voting System

The University Voting System is a secure, electronic platform designed specifically for university elections. Our system enables students, faculty, and staff to participate in campus democratic processes conveniently while maintaining the highest standards of security and integrity.

Whether you're voting in student government elections, department leadership contests, or university-wide referendums, this guide will help you understand how to use the platform effectively.

1.2 Key Features

Secure Voting

End-to-end encryption and blockchain verification ensure the integrity of every vote.

Anonymous Ballots

Sophisticated cryptographic protocols separate your identity from your voting choices.

Accessible Platform

Vote from any device with internet access—desktop, tablet, or mobile.

Verifiable Results

Confirm your vote was counted correctly using your unique receipt code.

Candidate Profiles

Learn about candidates with detailed profiles and platform information.

Real-time Results

View election outcomes as soon as they're verified and published.

2. Getting Started

2.1 System Requirements

The University Voting System is a web-based platform that works on most modern devices and browsers. Here are the minimum requirements:

Supported Browsers:

  • Google Chrome (version 90+)
  • Mozilla Firefox (version 88+)
  • Apple Safari (version 14+)
  • Microsoft Edge (version 90+)

Supported Devices:

  • Desktop/Laptop computers (Windows, macOS, Linux)
  • Tablets (iOS 13+, Android 9+)
  • Smartphones (iOS 13+, Android 9+)

Important: JavaScript must be enabled in your browser for the platform to function correctly.

2.2 Creating Your Account

1

Navigate to the Registration Page

Visit the registration page by clicking "Log In / Register" in the top navigation bar and then selecting "Create Account."

2

Enter Your University Email

You must use your official university email address ending with your institution's domain (e.g., @university.edu).

3

Verify Your Email

Check your university email inbox for a verification link. Click the link to confirm your email address. The verification link expires after 24 hours.

4

Complete Your Profile

After verifying your email, you'll be prompted to:

  • Create a secure password (at least 12 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols)
  • Enter your full name as it appears in university records
  • Provide your student/faculty ID number
  • Select your department or school
  • Indicate your class year or status (for students)

Tip: Make sure your information matches exactly what's in your university's official records to ensure verification goes smoothly.

5

Wait for Verification

Your account information will be automatically verified against your university's records. This process typically takes minutes but can take up to 24 hours in some cases. You'll receive an email notification once your account is fully verified and ready to use.

2.3 Logging In

Once your account is verified, you can log in to the platform:

  1. Visit the login page
  2. Enter your university email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Log In"

Note: For security reasons, you'll be automatically logged out after 30 minutes of inactivity. If you're voting in an election, make sure to complete your ballot within this timeframe or save your progress.
